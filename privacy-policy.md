## Overview

Daymaker Connect ("the Service") is an AI-powered network intelligence platform that helps professionals understand and leverage their LinkedIn connections. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

**The short version:** We don't sell your data. We don't share your data. Your information is used only to provide you with the Service.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Name, email address, and password when you create an account, or profile information from Google if you sign in with Google OAuth.
- **LinkedIn connection data:** When you upload your LinkedIn data export (CSV file), we store the contact information contained in that file, including names, companies, positions, email addresses, LinkedIn profile URLs, and connection dates.
- **North Star goal:** A professional goal statement you optionally provide to personalize AI recommendations.
- **Calendar data:** If you connect your Google or Microsoft calendar, we read (but never modify) your upcoming event titles, dates, locations, and attendee lists.
- **Event and attendee information:** Names and details you provide when creating event briefings.

### Information We Generate
- **AI-generated content:** Categories, conversation starters, event briefings, Deep Dive analyses, and monthly briefings generated by AI based on your data.
- **Vector embeddings:** Mathematical representations of your contact data used for AI search functionality. These are derived from data you provide and are not shared externally.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data:** How you interact with the Service (pages visited, features used, query counts).
- **Technical data:** Browser type, device information, and IP address for security and performance purposes.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Service:

- **To deliver core features:** Importing and organizing your LinkedIn connections, generating AI-powered insights, creating event briefings, and running Deep Dive analyses.
- **To personalize your experience:** Using your North Star goal, Reflections Match persona data (if connected), and usage patterns to tailor AI recommendations.
- **To process payments:** Managing your subscription through Stripe.
- **To communicate with you:** Sending service-related notifications such as monthly briefing availability.
- **To maintain and improve the Service:** Monitoring performance, fixing bugs, and developing new features.

### We Do NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your contact data with other users (your data is completely isolated)
- Use your data for advertising
- Allow third-party access to your information for their marketing purposes
- Scrape LinkedIn or any other platform on your behalf

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform. Each processes data only as necessary to provide their specific function:

- **Google Firebase:** Authentication and database storage (Google Cloud Platform)
- **Anthropic (Claude AI):** Processes your contact data to generate AI-powered insights, categorizations, and recommendations. Data is sent via API and is not stored by Anthropic for training purposes per their data usage policy.
- **OpenAI:** Generates mathematical embeddings of contact profiles for AI search. Data is sent via API and is not stored by OpenAI for training purposes per their API data usage policy.
- **Stripe:** Processes subscription payments. We do not store your credit card information directly.
- **Google Calendar API / Microsoft Graph API:** Reads calendar data when you connect your calendar. We request read-only access and never create, modify, or delete calendar events.

---

## 4. Data Isolation and Multi-Tenancy

Daymaker Connect is a multi-tenant application. Your data is strictly isolated:

- Your LinkedIn contacts, AI-generated insights, event briefings, and all other data are accessible only to your account.
- No other user can see, search, or access your data.
- Our database security rules enforce this isolation at the infrastructure level.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained as long as your account is active.
- **LinkedIn contact data:** Retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your data by contacting us.
- **AI-generated content:** Retained as long as your account is active.
- **Payment information:** Managed by Stripe according to their retention policies. We store only your Stripe customer ID, not payment details.

If you delete your account, we will delete all associated data within 30 days.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Database access is controlled through Firebase security rules with per-user isolation
- API keys and secrets are stored in Google Cloud Secret Manager
- Third-party API keys you provide (such as Reflections Match) are encrypted at rest
- Server-side authentication is required for all data access

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** your data at any time through the Service
- **Export** your data by contacting us
- **Delete** your account and all associated data by contacting us
- **Disconnect** third-party integrations (calendars, Reflections Match) at any time through Settings

---

## 8. LinkedIn Data

Daymaker Connect processes LinkedIn data that you manually export from LinkedIn using their "Download Your Data" feature. We do not:

- Access LinkedIn's servers or APIs
- Scrape LinkedIn profiles or pages
- Automate any actions on LinkedIn
- Send messages or connection requests through LinkedIn

All LinkedIn data in the Service originates from your voluntary CSV export.
